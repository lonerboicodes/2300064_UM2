PURPOSE   OF   SPLASH   PAGE

The purpose of the splash page is to introduce users to my website and provide a navigation bar to get them anywhere within it. It also contains my contact information for a few social media platforms.


PURPOSE   OF   PROFESSIONAL   PAGE

The Professional page was made with the intent of showcasing my skills and capabilities, as well as give brief background information of my education history and my goals as a person.


PURPOSE   OF   PERSONAL   PAGE

The Personal page contains information about me such as my likes and dislikes, as well as my hobbies and traits, along with one of my favorite quotes and the results of a personality test. It's purpose was to get viewers to know a little about me from a personal standpoint.


PURPOSE   OF   DEVELOPMENT   PAGE

The purpose of my development page was to highlight the meaningful message of the novel, Twelve Pillars, by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener. As well as how I can apply what I've read to life.


PURPOSE   OF   REFERENCE   PAGE

The reference page was created to give credit to all the sources I used to make my website into a reality.


PURPOSE   OF   STORYBOARD   PAGE

The Storyboard page is a key planning tool that combines visual elements and narrative structure to communicate ideas and project flow. In both creative and technical fields, it helps ensure that everyone is aligned on the overall concept and flow before proceeding to more complex stages of development or production.


THEME   OF   THE   SITE

The website features a dark theme for the Community page, creating an atmosphere where users feel like they're watching from the shadows. It's designed to be simplistic and free of clutter to enhance this effect, while also allowing for a subtle self-promotion.

The Professional page mirrors this aesthetic, maintaining simplicity to highlight key information. It presents concise details, ensuring that business visitors can quickly find what they need, and includes a background image of a reliable figure known for getting things done.

On the Personal page, an image representing duality reflects the creator's complex personality. Information is arranged on opposite sides to convey a 'good side' and a 'bad side.

Finally, the Development page features a dreamy ambiance that aligns with the overall dark theme, keeping it subdued rather than overly bright.
